🇧🇷 The Brazilian Alphabet

Brazilian Portuguese uses the same 26 letters as English. The biggest difference is pronunciation.

Letter Pronunciation Example
A ah Amor (Love)
B beh Bola (Ball)
C seh Casa (House)
D deh Dado (Dice)
E eh Escola (School)
F eh-fee Família (Family)
G zheh Gato (Cat)
H ah-gah Hotel
I ee Igreja (Church)
J zhoh-tah Janela (Window)
K kah Kiwi
L eh-lee Livro (Book)
M eh-mee Mãe (Mother)
N eh-nee Navio (Ship)
O oh Olho (Eye)
P peh Pão (Bread)
Q keh Queijo (Cheese)
R eh-hee Rua (Street)
S eh-see Sol (Sun)
T teh Tempo (Time)
U oo Uva (Grape)
V veh Vida (Life)
W dah-boo-lyoo Website
X shees Xícara (Cup)
Y eep-see-lon Yoga
Z zeh Zebra

💡 Pronunciation Tip

Although Brazilian Portuguese uses the same alphabet as English, many letters have completely different names and sounds. Practice saying each letter aloud before moving on to the next lesson.
