Lesson Notes

The Brazilian alphabet contains 26 letters, just like English. However, pronunciation is different. In this lesson you will learn:

  • Alphabet pronunciation
  • Brazilian sounds
  • Common mistakes
  • Listening practice

Practice

Exercise 1

Repeat the alphabet aloud.

Exercise 2

Match each letter with its pronunciation.

Exercise 3

Listen and repeat.
