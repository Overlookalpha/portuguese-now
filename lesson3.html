👋 Greetings

Greetings are one of the first things you should learn in any language. In this lesson you will discover the most common greetings used every day in Brazil.

Common Greetings

English Portuguese Audio
Hello Olá
Hi Oi
Good morning Bom dia
Good afternoon Boa tarde
Good evening / Good night Boa noite
Goodbye Tchau
See you later Até logo
See you tomorrow Até amanhã

💡 Tip

"Oi" is informal and commonly used among friends. "Olá" works in almost every situation.

Formal vs Informal Greetings

Greeting When to Use
Oi Friends, family and informal situations.
Olá Suitable for both formal and informal conversations.
Bom dia Used from early morning until around noon.
Boa tarde Used after lunch until the evening.
Boa noite Used in the evening and before going to bed.

🇧🇷 Brazilian Culture

Brazilians are generally friendly and enjoy greeting people warmly. It is common to smile, shake hands in formal situations, and greet friends with a hug or a kiss on the cheek, depending on the region. Using the correct greeting helps you make a good first impression.

Mini Dialogue

Maria: Olá!

João: Oi!

Maria: Bom dia!

João: Tudo bem?

Maria: Tudo bem, e você?

João: Tudo ótimo!
