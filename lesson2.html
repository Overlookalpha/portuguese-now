🗣️ Brazilian Pronunciation

One of the biggest differences between English and Brazilian Portuguese is pronunciation. Although both languages use the Latin alphabet, many letters sound completely different. By the end of this lesson you will be able to pronounce the most common Brazilian Portuguese sounds with confidence.

The Five Brazilian Vowels

Letter Sound Example
A ah Amor (Love)
E eh Escola (School)
I ee Ilha (Island)
O oh Ovo (Egg)
U oo Uva (Grape)

💡 Tip

Unlike English, Brazilian Portuguese vowels are pronounced clearly almost every time. Try saying each vowel slowly before listening to the examples.

Special Brazilian Sounds

Letters Sound Example
NH Like "ny" in canyon 🌅 Manhã
LH Like "lli" in million 👦 Filho
CH Like "sh" in shop 🌧️ Chuva
RR Like the "H" in "house" 🚗 Carro
Ç Like the "s" in "sun" 🫀 Coração

The Letter R

The Brazilian Portuguese letter R can have different sounds depending on where it appears in the word.

Example Pronunciation
🚗 Carro Strong "H" sound
😊 Cara Soft rolling R
🐭 Rato Strong "H" sound

The Letter S

The letter S can sound different depending on where it appears in the word.

Example Pronunciation
☀️ Sol Like the "S" in "Sun"
🏠 Casa Like the "Z" in "Zoo"
🚌 Ônibus Soft "S" at the end

The Letter X

The letter X is one of the most difficult letters in Brazilian Portuguese because it can have different sounds.

Example Pronunciation
📦 Caixa Like "SH"
🐟 Peixe Like "SH"
🚕 Táxi Like "KS"

Common Pronunciation Mistakes

Many English speakers make the same pronunciation mistakes when learning Brazilian Portuguese. Here are some of the most common ones:

  • ❌ Saying "R" like English. ✅ Use the Brazilian R sound.
  • ❌ Ignoring accents (á, é, ê, ó...). ✅ Accents change pronunciation.
  • ❌ Pronouncing every "S" the same way. ✅ Listen to the context.
  • ❌ Pronouncing "NH" as N + H. ✅ It has its own unique sound.
  • ❌ Forgetting to open the vowels. ✅ Brazilian Portuguese vowels are usually very clear.

🎯 Pronunciation Tips

  • ✅ Speak slowly. Accuracy is more important than speed.
  • ✅ Listen first, then repeat.
  • ✅ Repeat each new word at least 5 times.
  • ✅ Record your voice and compare it with the audio.
  • ✅ Practice for 10 minutes every day instead of one long session each week.

🎧 Listening Practice

Listen to each sound and repeat it aloud at least three times. Speaking out loud is one of the fastest ways to improve your pronunciation.

Lesson Notes

Brazilian Portuguese pronunciation is very consistent. Most words are pronounced exactly as they are written. Learning the vowel sounds and special letter combinations will help you understand almost every new word you learn.

  • ✔️ Learn the five vowels.
  • ✔️ Practice NH, LH, CH, RR and Ç.
  • ✔️ Listen before speaking.
  • ✔️ Repeat words several times.
