Food & Drinks

💡 Tip

To talk about food in Portuguese, you will often use the expressions eu gosto de (I like) and eu quero (I want).

Eu gosto de café. (I like coffee.)

Eu gosto de arroz e feijão. (I like rice and beans.)

Eu quero água. (I want water.)

Eu quero pão. (I want bread.)

🍽️ Food in Sentences

Eu gosto de café.
(I like coffee.)

Eu quero água.
(I want water.)

Ela come arroz e feijão.
(She eats rice and beans.)

Nós bebemos suco.
(We drink juice.)

🇧🇷 Brazilian Culture

Rice and beans are the most traditional everyday meal in Brazil. Brazil is also famous for pão de queijo, açaí, tropical fruits, fresh juices and excellent coffee. Sharing meals with family and friends is an important part of Brazilian culture.

🎧 Listening Practice

Repeat these sentences aloud:

  • Eu gosto de café.
  • Eu quero água.
  • Ela come arroz e feijão.
  • Nós bebemos suco.
