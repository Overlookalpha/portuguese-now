Shopping

💡 Useful Expressions

These expressions are commonly used when shopping in Brazil.

Quanto custa? (How much does it cost?)

Quanto é? (How much is it?)

Posso pagar com cartão? (Can I pay with a card?)

Tem desconto? (Is there a discount?)

Eu vou levar. (I'll take it.)

🛍️ Shopping Dialogue

Cliente: Boa tarde! Quanto custa esta camisa?
(Good afternoon! How much does this shirt cost?)

Vendedor: Ela custa cem reais.
(It costs one hundred reais.)

Cliente: Tem desconto?
(Is there a discount?)

Vendedor: Sim! Hoje está com dez por cento de desconto.
(Yes! Today it's ten percent off.)

Cliente: Ótimo! Eu vou levar.
(Great! I'll take it.)

Vendedor: Vai pagar com cartão ou dinheiro?
(Will you pay with card or cash?)

Cliente: Cartão de crédito, por favor.
(Credit card, please.)

Vendedor: Aqui está o recibo. Obrigado!
(Here is the receipt. Thank you!)

🇧🇷 Brazilian Culture

Shopping malls are very popular in Brazil and are commonly called "Shopping". Many stores also offer installment payments (parcelamento) when paying with a credit card, especially for more expensive products.
