🔢 Numbers

Numbers are everywhere. You use them to tell your age, your phone number, prices, addresses, dates and much more. Let's learn the most important numbers first.

Numbers 0–10

Number Portuguese Audio
0 Zero
1 Um
2 Dois
3 Três
4 Quatro
5 Cinco
6 Seis
7 Sete
8 Oito
9 Nove
10 Dez

💡 Tip

Listen to each number carefully and repeat it aloud several times. Correct pronunciation is more important than speed.

💬 Real Life Examples

How old are you?

➡️ Eu tenho vinte anos.


What is your phone number?

➡️ Meu telefone é nove oito sete seis cinco quatro três dois um.


How much is it?

➡️ Custa vinte reais.

Brazilian Culture

In Brazil, prices are normally written using a comma instead of a period. For example: R$ 19,90 This means nineteen reais and ninety centavos.
