Colors

In this lesson you will learn the most common colors in Brazilian Portuguese and how to describe objects around you.

🎨 Colors in Sentences

O céu é azul.
(The sky is blue.)

A grama é verde.
(The grass is green.)

Meu carro é preto.
(My car is black.)

Minha camisa é branca.
(My shirt is white.)

🇧🇷 Brazilian Culture

Brazil is famous for its colorful culture. The Brazilian flag uses green, yellow, blue and white. The Carnival is one of the most colorful celebrations in the world.
