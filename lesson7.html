Colors

In this lesson you will learn the most common colors in Brazilian Portuguese and how to describe people, objects and places.

🎨 Vocabulary

English Portuguese Audio
Red Vermelho
Blue Azul
Green Verde
Yellow Amarelo
Orange Laranja
Purple Roxo
Pink Rosa
Brown Marrom
Black Preto
White Branco
Gray Cinza

💡 Tip

Many Portuguese colors change according to the gender of the noun they describe.

Carro vermelho (Red car)

Casa vermelha (Red house)

Gato preto (Black cat)

Camisa preta (Black shirt)

🎨 Colors in Sentences

O céu é azul.
(The sky is blue.)

A grama é verde.
(The grass is green.)

Meu carro é preto.
(My car is black.)

Minha camisa é branca.
(My shirt is white.)

🇧🇷 Brazilian Culture

Brazil is famous for its colorful culture. The Brazilian flag uses the colors green, yellow, blue and white. During Carnival, cities are filled with colorful costumes, decorations and music.

🎧 Listening Practice

Repeat these sentences aloud:

  • O céu é azul.
  • A banana é amarela.
  • A árvore é verde.
  • Meu carro é preto.
